How Dollar-Cost Averaging Works

Pick an Amount

Choose a fixed dollar amount you can afford regularly — $5, $50, $500. Consistency matters more than size.

Buy on Schedule

Buy daily, weekly, or monthly regardless of the price. You’ll buy more sats when the price is low, fewer when it’s high.

Time in Market

DCA smooths out volatility over time. Historically, longer stacking periods have rewarded patient Bitcoiners.
