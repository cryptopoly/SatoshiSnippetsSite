BITCOIN TIMELINE

Bitcoin Timeline

Every major milestone in Bitcoin's history, from the whitepaper to becoming a global financial asset.

2008-08-18Genesis

bitcoin.org Domain Registered

The domain bitcoin.org was registered via anonymousspeech.com.

2008-10-31Genesis

Bitcoin Whitepaper Published

Satoshi Nakamoto published 'Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System' on the cryptography mailing list.

2009-01-03Genesis

Genesis Block Mined

Block 0 mined with the message: 'The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks'

2009-01-09Genesis

Bitcoin v0.1 Released

The first version of the Bitcoin software was released to the cryptography mailing list.

2009-01-12Milestone

First Bitcoin Transaction

Satoshi sent 10 BTC to Hal Finney in block 170 - the first ever person-to-person Bitcoin transaction.

2009-10-05Milestone

First Exchange Rate

New Liberty Standard established the first Bitcoin exchange rate: 1,309.03 BTC = $1 USD, based on electricity cost of mining.

2010-05-22Milestone

Bitcoin Pizza Day

Laszlo Hanyecz paid 10,000 BTC for two Papa John's pizzas - the first known real-world Bitcoin transaction. Worth ~$41 at the time.

2010-07-11Adoption

Bitcoin v0.3 on Slashdot

Bitcoin v0.3 was mentioned on Slashdot, bringing a huge wave of new users and interest.

2010-07-17Adoption

Mt. Gox Exchange Launches

The Mt. Gox Bitcoin exchange launched, becoming the dominant exchange for several years.

2010-08-15Technical

Value Overflow Bug

A critical bug created 184 billion BTC in a single transaction. Satoshi quickly released a patch and the chain was forked.

2010-12-12Satoshi

Satoshi's Last Forum Post

Satoshi made his final post on BitcoinTalk about DoS prevention, then disappeared from public view.

2011-02-09Milestone

Bitcoin Reaches $1 USD

Bitcoin achieved parity with the US dollar for the first time.

2011-04-23Satoshi

Satoshi's Last Known Email

Satoshi's last known communication was an email to Gavin Andresen, asking him not to portray Satoshi as mysterious.

2011-06-01Adoption

WikiLeaks Accepts Bitcoin

WikiLeaks began accepting Bitcoin donations after being cut off from traditional payment processors.

2012-11-28Halving

First Halving

Block reward reduced from 50 BTC to 25 BTC at block 210,000. Price was ~$12.

2013-03-28Milestone

Market Cap Hits $1 Billion

Bitcoin's market capitalization exceeded $1 billion for the first time.

2013-11-29Milestone

BTC Hits $1,000

Bitcoin price reached $1,000 for the first time, driven by Chinese demand.

2014-02-24Milestone

Mt. Gox Collapse

Mt. Gox filed for bankruptcy after losing ~850,000 BTC. A pivotal moment that led to better security practices.

2014-03-07Satoshi

Satoshi: 'I Am Not Dorian Nakamoto'

After Newsweek claimed to have found Satoshi, the real Satoshi posted his final known message on P2P Foundation.

2015-01-26Adoption

Coinbase Launches US Exchange

Coinbase launched the first licensed US Bitcoin exchange, bringing mainstream accessibility.

2016-07-09Halving

Second Halving

Block reward reduced from 25 BTC to 12.5 BTC at block 420,000. Price was ~$650.

2017-08-01Technical

Bitcoin Cash Fork

Bitcoin Cash (BCH) forked from Bitcoin over the block size debate. BTC maintained the original chain.

2017-08-23Technical

SegWit Activates

Segregated Witness soft fork activated, fixing transaction malleability and enabling the Lightning Network.

2017-12-17Milestone

BTC Hits $20,000

Bitcoin reached its then all-time high of nearly $20,000 during a massive bull run.

2018-01-13Technical

Lightning Network Launches

The first Lightning Network implementations went live on Bitcoin mainnet, enabling instant micropayments.

2019-06-18Adoption

Facebook Announces Libra

Facebook's Libra announcement brought global attention to cryptocurrency and spurred regulatory discussions.

2020-05-11Halving

Third Halving

Block reward reduced from 12.5 BTC to 6.25 BTC at block 630,000. Price was ~$8,600.

2020-08-11Adoption

MicroStrategy Buys Bitcoin

MicroStrategy became the first public company to adopt Bitcoin as its primary treasury reserve asset.

2021-02-08Adoption

Tesla Buys $1.5B in Bitcoin

Tesla announced a $1.5 billion Bitcoin purchase, sending the price soaring.

2021-06-09Adoption

El Salvador Legal Tender

El Salvador became the first country to adopt Bitcoin as legal tender.

2021-11-10Milestone

BTC Hits $69,000

Bitcoin reached a new all-time high of approximately $69,000.

2021-11-14Technical

Taproot Upgrade Activates

The Taproot soft fork activated, bringing Schnorr signatures, MAST, and improved privacy to Bitcoin.

2024-01-10Adoption

Spot Bitcoin ETFs Approved

The SEC approved 11 spot Bitcoin ETFs, opening Bitcoin to traditional investors through familiar vehicles.

2024-04-19Halving

Fourth Halving

Block reward reduced from 6.25 BTC to 3.125 BTC at block 840,000.

2024-12-05Milestone

BTC Hits $100,000

Bitcoin crossed the historic $100,000 mark for the first time.

2025-01-20Adoption

US Strategic Bitcoin Reserve

Executive order signed establishing a strategic Bitcoin reserve for the United States.
