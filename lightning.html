Lightning Firsts

Feb 2015Poon–Dryja whitepaper

Joseph Poon and Thaddeus Dryja publish "The Bitcoin Lightning Network" — scalable off-chain payments via payment channels.

Jan 2016Lightning Labs founded

Elizabeth Stark, Olaoluwa Osuntokun and team begin building lnd, the most widely used Lightning implementation.

Dec 2017First mainnet transaction

The first real Lightning payment on Bitcoin mainnet is broadcast — the network goes from paper to production.

Jan 2018Bitrefill first commercial use

Bitrefill enables Lightning payments for gift cards and phone top-ups — the first commercial merchant on LN.

Jan 2019Lightning Torch

A global relay of a growing Lightning payment is passed between users, eventually reaching Jack Dorsey.

Oct 2020Wumbo channels

Lightning removes the 0.16 BTC channel cap, unlocking large-capacity routing and enterprise use cases.

Sep 2021El Salvador adopts Bitcoin

The Chivo wallet brings Lightning to millions, making El Salvador the first country with Bitcoin + LN as legal tender.

Apr 2022Taproot Assets announced

Lightning Labs unveils Taro (now Taproot Assets) to issue and transfer assets over Lightning.

Jul 2023Splicing ships

Phoenix wallet enables splicing in production — adding or removing funds from a channel without closing it.

Mar 2024BOLT12 offers adoption

Reusable payment requests (offers) gain traction across wallets, improving UX for recurring payments.

The entire Lightning Network was once bootstrapped from a single node running on a laptop — today it routes thousands of BTC.
